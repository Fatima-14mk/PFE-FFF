Why and How to Donate?

Do you have tools that are no longer in use? You can give them a second life by donating them!

Across the globe, millions of people with disabilities face challenges due to a lack of essential resources. While we can't meet every need alone, you can make a difference. By donating, you bring mobility, independence, and hope to someone who needs it, transforming not only their life but also that of their family.

You can support in two ways:

  • Donate equipment and essential items Mobility aids, assistive devices, and other useful tools can greatly improve daily life.
